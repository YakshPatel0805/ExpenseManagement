## How to Delete Your Account

### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Navigate to Settings
1. Log into your account
2. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
3. Scroll down to **Data & Privacy** section

#### Step 2: Click Delete Button
1. Find **Delete Account** option
2. Click the red **Delete** button

#### Step 3: First Confirmation
You'll see a warning dialog:

```
‚ö†Ô∏è WARNING: This will permanently delete your account and ALL data!

This includes:
‚Ä¢ All expenses and income records
‚Ä¢ All accounts/wallets
‚Ä¢ All transactions

- Click **OK** to continue
- Click **Cancel** to abort

#### Step 4: Type Confirmation
You must type exactly: `DELETE MY ACCOUNT`

```
To confirm deletion, please type: DELETE MY ACCOUNT

(Type exactly as shown, in capital letters)
```

**Important:**
- Must be in CAPITAL LETTERS
- Must match exactly (no extra spaces)
- Case-sensitive

#### Step 5: Final Confirmation
One last warning:

```
This is your FINAL warning!

Are you absolutely sure you want to delete your account?

Click OK to permanently delete everything, or Cancel to keep your account.
```

- Click **OK** to proceed with deletion
- Click **Cancel** to abort

#### Step 6: Account Deleted
If successful, you'll see:

```

You'll be automatically logged out and redirected to the home page.

## Troubleshooting

### "Error deleting account"
**Possible causes:**
- Network connection issue
- Server temporarily unavailable
- Session expired

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

### "Text did not match"
**Cause:** You didn't type `DELETE MY ACCOUNT` exactly

**Solution:**
- Type in CAPITAL LETTERS
- No extra spaces
- Exact spelling

### Accidentally Deleted
**Unfortunately:** There is no recovery option

**What you can do:**
1. Create a new account
2. Import your exported data (if you saved it)
3. Manually re-enter data (if no export)
